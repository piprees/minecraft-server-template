Welcome to the server. These are the community rules.

1. Treat everyone with respect. Absolutely no harassment, witch hunting, sexism, racism or hate speech will be tolerated.
2. No spam or self-promotion (server invites, advertisements etc) without permission from a staff member. This includes DMing fellow members.
3. If you see something against the rules or something that makes you feel unsafe, let staff know.
4. No age-restricted or obscene content. This includes text, images or links featuring nudity, sex, hard violence or other disturbing graphic content.
5. Don't grief or steal from others. Use the land claim system to protect your builds.
6. Don't use exploits, duplication glitches, or x-ray.
7. Keep the landscape tidy, don't leave floating trees or 1x1 towers.
8. If you find someone's build, don't modify it without permission.
9. PvP only if both players agree.
10. Have fun exploring, building, and adventuring together.
