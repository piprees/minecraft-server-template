Getting started

1. Register on Discord. Join the Discord server, type /register YourMinecraftUsername in #minecraft, then ask an admin for the @Player role.

2. Install Prism Launcher. Download it here. It's free, handles mods, and you only need it once.

3. Import the modpack. Download the pack from the button above, then in Prism: Add Instance > Import > Browse, pick the file. Or paste this URL into Prism's Import tab instead:

4. Play. Add your Microsoft account in Prism (top right), double-click the instance, done. Mods download on first launch and sync automatically every time you play.

What do I need to play?

You need a paid copy of Minecraft: Java Edition. If you don't have one, go to minecraft.net, sign in with a Microsoft account, and buy Minecraft: Java & Bedrock Edition for PC.

Your computer needs at least 8 GB of RAM. 16 GB is better.

Voice chat

Proximity voice chat is built in. Stand near someone and talk. Voice activation and noise suppression are already on.

Press V to open voice settings: pick your mic, adjust volumes, or switch to push-to-talk.

Maps
  • Minimap: always in the corner, shows nearby terrain and players.
  • World map (M): everywhere you've explored. Press B to drop a waypoint.
  • Web map: map.${DOMAIN} shows the world in 3D in your browser.

To pre-fill your minimap with everything already explored, open chat in game and type: /maplink download_tiles 2048 2048 0 0. Click the map name when asked, open the full map (M), and wait for it to fill in.

Shaders & Distant Horizons

The pack comes with a shader for nicer lighting, water, and shadows. If it's slow, turn it off: Options > Video > Shader Packs > OFF.

Distant Horizons lets you see terrain far beyond normal render distance. Keep your normal render distance low (8-12). DH handles everything beyond that.

If far-away terrain looks weird at night, set DH's "LOD Shading" to "Old Lighting / Forced", or turn the shader off.

Staying up to date

One-click instance: the pack checks for updates and syncs itself every time you launch. Your keybinds and settings are never touched.

Manual install: download the latest .mrpack from this page, then right-click your instance > Edit > Modrinth > Update from file. If things get weird after an update, switch to the one-click instance.
